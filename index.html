A Smart Bracelet for an Easier life.

We designed this product to offer a solution for two major problems a person with hearing impairment faces each day.

  • - Avoiding Dangers
  • - Improving Communications

We also offer a mobile app which connects with the smart bracelet and allow you to configure its settings easily.

Avoiding Dangers

We use cutting-edge deep learning techniques to detect sounds generated by alert systems then notify the user using vibration which otherwise would be unheard.

Additionaly, it can detect other day-to-day sounds like alarms.

Improved Communication

We use cutting-edge deep learning techniques to detect and transform speech into written text.

Our product can even detect and separate speech when multiple people are talking at the same time then displays the text into neat colored bubbles on the screen.
